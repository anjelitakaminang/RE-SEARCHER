About Researcher Recommendation System

A recommendation system is a system or application created to be able to provide and provide recommendations for an item to make a decision desired by the system user. A recommendation system is a tool and technique in Artificial Intelligence software (software) that can provide suggestions to users for items that might be useful for users in making choices. Meanwhile, the definition of Artificial Intelligence (AI) generally means computer artificial intelligence that can imitate human intellectual abilities. AI-Powered Recommendations in recommendation systems are systems that use artificial intelligence techniques such as deep learning, neural networks, and other machine learning algorithms to increase the accuracy and personalization of recommendations
